My background

Hey, I'm Sem, 20 years old and a second-year student studying Communication & Multimedia Design. I would describe myself as a social, passionate and ambitious designer / developer. I am constantly looking for new ways to improve my skills and challenge myself.

My strength lies in looking at problems in a critical and analytical way and then solving them with new concepts and creative ideas. I enjoy working with others working together. Giving good constructive feedback and creating a pleasant group feeling are therefore very important to me. to me. I am convinced that in this this way, together with others, you can make a final product that is many times better than the sum of its parts.

I also enjoy working on meaningful projects. Projects where I can fully empathise with the target group to better understand their wishes and desires better. Projects that not only only ensure satisfied customers and happy users, but also make the world a slightly better place.
