Description

Limburg Leeft is a 'hypothetical' which celebrates the history of Limburg. We chose 5 important from it's history as focal points and designed a poster series around them. After conceptualizing and creating this poster series, we had a good foundation to build on and continued to make different assets for the event.

We created a website, a poster series, a map, a ticket, armbands, banners and of course beer. All of these assets were designed with the same style in mind, which we created in the poster series.

My responsibilities

Together with my teammate we conceptualized and designed the first 5 posters. Afterwards we split the responsibilities. I made the website, banners and ticket.
