Description

The CBS (Central Bureau of Statistics) asked us to create an interactive and engaging multimedia story about agriculture. The question we had to answer was the following: 'What makes the Netherlands unique when it comes to agriculture?'. One of these things is our heavy use of A.I. to calculate the optimal growing environments of different plants.

This inspired us to create 'Agra'. An A.I. which tells the story of how dutch techniques can prevent future famines around the globe.

My responsibilities

My main responsibility was developing the website. I created a simple system, which uses a JSON file to store and change between the different scenes of the story.
